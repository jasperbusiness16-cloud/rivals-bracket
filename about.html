OUR MISSION

Built By Competitors, For Competitors

Rivals Gauntlet was created by two brothers with a shared passion for competitive gaming and community-driven events. Our goal is to provide organized, high-quality Marvel Rivals tournaments that give players a place to compete, improve, and be part of something bigger.


Whether you're entering your first tournament or competing for the championship, we're committed to creating events that are competitive, fair, and entertaining for both players and viewers.
