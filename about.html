What We Are

More Than a Tournament Bracket

Rivals Gauntlet brings competition, broadcast coverage, predictions and player progression together in one connected platform. Competitors are not simply entering isolated events—they are building a permanent record across the Gauntlet.

Every tournament contributes to a larger competitive ecosystem built around players, viewers and community participation.
Our Mission

Built by Competitors, for Competitors

Our goal is to create fair, entertaining and professionally presented Marvel Rivals events that welcome new competitors while still giving serious teams something meaningful to pursue.

The platform will continue expanding with deeper tournament systems, predictions, rankings, rewards and community-driven events.
