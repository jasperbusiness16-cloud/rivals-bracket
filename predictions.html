Prediction Command Protocol

How It Works

1

Build the complete bracket

Quarterfinal winners populate the Semifinals. Semifinal winners populate the Grand Final and your final choice becomes the predicted champion.

2

Submit before the tournament begins

The full bracket locks when the first matchup becomes currentMatch. Submitted brackets can be updated until that moment.

3

Chase a perfect bracket

Every matchup must be correct to earn the exclusive Perfect Bracket reward. The remaining counter updates as official results are finalized.

4

Predict the upcoming live match

Live questions open when staff set nextMatch. Saved answers remain selected and lock automatically when that matchup becomes currentMatch.

5

Advance your predictor career

Accuracy determines rank. Long-term participation increases Predictor Level and unlocks a capped reward multiplier for standard prediction rewards.
